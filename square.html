Hollywood Squares: Game Studies

A single-file classroom game for GitHub Pages. Play up to 10 rounds, click any square to open the prompt, reveal the answer when ready, and claim the square for X or O. Built for easy teacher control and fast class discussion.

How to Use It

Click a square. Ask the prompt. Reveal the answer if needed. Claim the square for X or O. Award points if you want. Win the round by making three in a row, or just use the board as a whole-class discussion game.

Teacher Reminder

Keep the follow-up where the learning happens. After a team answers, make them explain why, connect the idea to a game or assignment, or improve the answer into a stronger analytical claim.
