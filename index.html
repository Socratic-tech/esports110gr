How This Tool Works

Each Training Module

You'll work through five core review sections. For each one:

  • Model: Study a professional review excerpt
  • Explain: Learn why reviewers write this way
  • Practice: Draft your own outline notes

You're in Control

Work through sections in order, or jump to what interests you. You can take your time—there's no timer, no score, and no pressure.

Your outline notes stay with you until you're ready to export them for your actual review.
