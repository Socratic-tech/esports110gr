How This System Works

Four Phases, One Goal

You'll move through four connected phases:

  • Phase 0: Propose your game (justify your choice)
  • Phase 1: Study professional reviews (learn the structure)
  • Phase 2: Build your outline (plan your review)
  • Phase 3: Write your review (create final product)

All Your Data, Your Device

Everything you type is saved to your browser (not to a server). You own your work. When you're ready, download or copy to Word/Google Docs and submit through Canvas.

Your instructor only sees what you submit—not your drafts or thinking process in these tools.

Ready to Get Started?

Step 1: Start with Game Proposal (Phase 0) to choose and justify your game. Submit through Canvas.

Step 2: While waiting for feedback, complete the optional Training Quest (Phase 1) to learn professional review structure.

Step 3: Once your proposal is approved, use the Review Outline Builder (Phase 2) to plan your detailed review. Submit outline through Canvas.

Step 4: Use your approved outline to write or produce your final review (Phase 3).
